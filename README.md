#Brazil E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

##Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Business Intelligence dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze sales performance of a Brazilian e-commerce dataset.

The goal of this dashboard is to provide insights into:

* Revenue trends over time
* Customer purchasing behavior
* Regional sales distribution
* Product performance

---

##Business Objectives

* Identify **key revenue drivers**
* Analyze **top-performing products and customers**
* Understand **geographical distribution of sales**
* Track **growth trends over time**

---

##Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** (Data visualization & dashboarding)
* **Power Query** (Data transformation)
* **DAX** (Calculated measures)

---

##Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** 13.59M
* **Total Orders:** 99.44K
* **Total Customers:** 99.44K

---

##Dashboard Features

* **Revenue Over Time**

  * Tracks sales growth from 2016 to 2018

* **Revenue by State**

  * Highlights top-performing regions (SP, RJ, MG)

* **Top Products by Revenue**

  * Identifies best-selling products

* **Top Customers**

  * Shows high-value customers contributing to revenue

---

##Key Insights

* Revenue shows strong growth between 2016 and 2018
* São Paulo (SP) is the leading state in revenue generation
*  A small group of products contributes significantly to total sales
*  Revenue is concentrated among top customers

---

## Limitations

* Customer identification is based on IDs (no segmentation applied yet)
* Limited behavioral analysis (e.g., churn, retention not included)
